Game Description

Immersive Underwater Experience An innovative project designed to immerse players in an interactive underwater world using A-Frame. Users explore a 3D ocean environment as divers, collecting magical bubbles floating along underwater currents. Key Features: Interactivity: Click on bubbles to capture them while exploring the ocean. Objective: Collect as many bubbles as possible to increase your score. Animations: Bubbles vanish with visual effects upon interaction. Perfect for showcasing how technology turns imagination into captivating, unique experiences.
